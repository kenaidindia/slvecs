Requirements to Become a Pharmaceutical Scientist

To find a respectful job as a pharmaceutical scientist a 4 year bachelor degree is needed. It is recomended to major in cheistry or chemical engineering if the college you are attending does not offer pharmaceutical sciences as a major. Classes that are important to this field include, pharmacology, chemistry, anatomy, biology, and mathematics.
While some jobs will accept just 4 years in school a masters or doctoral degree is greatly valued. This is 4-6 years of schooling and typically research experiance is necessary.
For students thinking about this carrer it is important for them to know most of the job is gathering, analyzing, and understanding medical data.
