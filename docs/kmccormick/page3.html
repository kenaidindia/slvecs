Job Options and Benefits

Inside pharmaceutical companies there are many jobs to fill. The main decision you need to make is if you want to be in the discovery, development, or manufacturing of the drugs getting developed.
There are many departments inside a company and can be placed in one of these three areas. The first is research and development where scientists are in the lab finding molecules to improve medications or create new ones. Next is reglatory affairs where you keep track if all the regulations are being followed so the medication can be sold to the public. Then, validation which is the job tracking how the medicine is made so it can be reproduced to be sold. Another area inside a company is production, these people are making the medicine that is being used worldwide. Finally, there is quality control, where they check the manufacturing process to make sure the product is safe.
