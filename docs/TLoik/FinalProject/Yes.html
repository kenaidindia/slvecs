The first reason people might download music illegally is because they do not have enough money. Many young people are too young to get a job and their parents will not pay for them to buy music. Also the music they are trying to buy could be very expensive and out of their price range.

The second reason that people might illegally download music is that people do not want to pay for the music they are downloading.
