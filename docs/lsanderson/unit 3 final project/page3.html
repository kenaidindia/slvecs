Let us say that the subject decides to shift the lever to save the group
of five and the train runs over the one person. Again I will show 3 reasons
for this side of the dilemma.

Reason 1

Although we are talking about human lives from a utilitarian point
of view this path is a no-brainer because "our duty is to promote
the greatest happiness of the greatest number". Five lives saved is
better than one life saved.

Reason 2

The fact that you are trying to promote some greater good outways the
harm as it is only a side effect to your good intent.

Reason 3

"Studies show that the majority of people today, at least in modern
Western societies say that they would pull the lever." Given this,
this choice is popularly chosen so more likely than not you probably
agree with this choice.
