The Trolley Problem is an ethical dilemma on whether it is more ethical to have five people die
without you interfering with anyone's death or flipping a switch to save the five but end up
killing one person.

Many have debated which is the "corrct choice" and a lot also stating what they think they would do. But like many other things putting it into practice is a lot more difficult than theory. Below Michael Stevens from Vsauce
