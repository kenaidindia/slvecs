Computer Programming

salary benefits, and where this job wil get you in ten years. The whole point of this website is to bring awareness
about this job and hopefully create more Computer Programmers.

I will have four different sites with each one explaining a different part of the Computer Programming career path.
One will be giving you a description of the Computer Programming job and another one explaining the pros anc cons of the job.
The third and fourth site will be telling you the salary benefits and where the job will get you in ten years.
